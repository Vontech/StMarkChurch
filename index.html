Welcome to our community!

Founded in 1895 and celebrating 121 years!

Our mission is to be a Christian community rooted in the Black religious experience and tradition that welcomes and affirms all God's children, serves our community, and is committed to the liberating gospel and ministry of Jesus Christ.


We will achieve this mission by accomplishing these goals:

  1. Relearning our sacred story and reclaiming our unique calling.
  2. Reaffirming our identity as a United Church of Christ church, rooted in cultural expression of the black worship experience.
  3. Reigniting our passion for community engagement.
  4. Reinvesting our financial resources.
  5. Renewing our unity as a congregation.
